Past CTFs and Hackathons

  • UCTF 2021  We gave it our all, doing well in the Forensics, and Web categories, but the RE and pwn were are downfall. It was a excellent event though, with some challenges for beginners. March,2021
  • darkCON 2021  The first CTF the team completed, we did very well, especially in the OSNIT and web challenges. It was a great event. Feb,2021
  • Union CTF 2021  We popped into this one for a bit. The OSINT and Forensics challenges were interesting.Feb,2021
  • Geek Seek CTF 2020  The first CTF the club ever competed in as a team. Summer,2020
  • Space Apps 2019 Hackathon  Our team were National Finalists in this hackathon. We created an application visualizing, and classifying electromagnetic data. Oct,2019
